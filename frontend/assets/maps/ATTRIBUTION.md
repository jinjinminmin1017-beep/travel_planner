- Source: https://ultimaps.com/blank-maps/world/

- Credit: Ultimaps.com

## world-map-flow.png

- Derived from `world-map.png`.
- Local processing: `world-map-flow.png` is generated from every non-transparent land pixel in `world-map.png`, recolored to teal, and used as the single water-flow planning mask so all blank land areas are covered from left to right without showing a rectangular overlay.
